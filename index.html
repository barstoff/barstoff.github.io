I am a joint PhD Researcher in Economics and Data Science at IMT School for Advanced Studies and KU Leuven. In 2019, I was a visiting scholar at Harvard University.

I am fascinated by the application of statistical methods to social and health sciences. The main fields of my investigation are causal inference, applied machine learning, public policy evaluation and political economy.

Currently, I am working on the applications of machine learning in social and data sciences. In particular, I study methods to improve public policies amid the rampant heterogeneity in the world

Previously, I have been a City Councillor and I got degrees in Statistics and Sociology.
